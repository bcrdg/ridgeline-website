About Us

Get to know the people behind Ridgeline IT Solutions.

Our Mission

At Ridgeline IT Solutions, our mission is to empower businesses with reliable, secure, and forward-thinking technology solutions. We aim to be more than just an IT provider — we are a trusted partner in helping businesses thrive in a digital-first world.

Community Commitment

We live here, shop here, and raise our families here — which is why giving back matters. Ridgeline offers discounted services to local nonprofits, seniors, and military families, hosts free security awareness trainings, and invests in youth and community programs that make the Mother Lode stronger.

Who We Serve

We partner with organizations across a wide range of industries — including real estate, healthcare, manufacturing, retail, hospitality, and state and local government. This breadth of experience means we understand both the specific needs of your sector and the broader technology ecosystem your business operates within.

Brian Carlson

Brian Carlson has over 18 years of professional IT experience and studied computer science at Chabot College. Brian began his career as a junior systems administrator for a large printing company, managing more than 300 computers across 20 locations in California. He later founded and operated SF Bay Computers before joining a larger IT company in the Bay Area. Today, Brian leads Ridgeline IT Solutions and is pursuing his CISSP certification to further validate his expertise in cybersecurity leadership, implementation, and management.

Beyond technology, Brian enjoys skiing, hiking, and camping in the Sierra Nevada. He is a father of three, a world traveler, and the founder of the California Wilderness Alliance, a nonprofit dedicated to preserving California’s natural spaces. He also restores classic arcade machines, practices juggling, and loves to cook — especially experimenting with sauces — bringing the same care and experimentation to the kitchen that he brings to his work in IT.
